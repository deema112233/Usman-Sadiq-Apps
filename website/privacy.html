Privacy Policy

Usman Sadiq Apps operates with a commitment to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our services.

1. Information Collection and Use

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us, such as your name, email address, and any other details required for the proper functioning of our services. This information is used solely to provide and improve our services.

Face Data

The Ranking Filter does not collect, store, or share users' face data. All processing, including applying filters and generating rankings, is done locally on the user’s device. This ensures that your photos, videos, or other personal information remain private and are never transmitted to external servers or third parties. Once the camera screen is off, all the data is wiped automatically. We need access to a camera and microphone to detect face, record video, and enable interactive features.
